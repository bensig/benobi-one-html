Short Bio

Ben Sigman is a Bitcoin entrepreneur, author, and advocate focused on global Bitcoin adoption. He conceived and leads Libre, Bitcoin’s first decentralized two-way lending marketplace.

He is the bestselling co-author of Bitcoin One Million, ranked #1 in Macroeconomics, Monetary Policy, and Bitcoin categories, and the co-creator of Bitcoin Alpha, the invite-only Bitcoin unconference series.

Ben also designed the giant orange shark sculpture, “Apex Predator of Money”—a bold symbol proposed as Bitcoin’s new mascot as featured in Decrypt.

With over 20 years in technology as a systems engineer and developer, his work bridges Bitcoin, art, and open finance to create a more accessible monetary future.
