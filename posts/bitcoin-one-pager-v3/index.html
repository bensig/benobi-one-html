Bitcoin One Pager Version 3: A Better Resource for Understanding Bitcoin

November 16, 2025

The Problem: No Great Starting Point

I’ve been thinking about this for a while - there’s really no great resource that I’m aware of for sending someone who is trying to get into Bitcoin or knows nothing about it. You know the conversation: a friend says “I still don’t understand it, man. I’ve been friends with you for years and I still don’t get Bitcoin.”

I wanted to create something better.

Version 3: Simpler and Deeper

I updated the Bitcoin one pager to include additional information while making it simpler in some ways. Page one is now even more accessible, while page two goes more in-depth with concepts that really matter.

The Difficulty Adjustment: The Key to Understanding Bitcoin’s Value

One of the most important additions to version 3 is an explanation of the difficulty adjustment and how mining works. I read somewhere that there’s no crossover between people who can’t explain the difficulty adjustment and people who don’t see value in Bitcoin.

In other words: if someone doesn’t see value in Bitcoin, they usually cannot explain how the difficulty adjustment works.

This really makes sense - it’s not the simplest concept, but it’s also not that hard to understand. It’s absolutely fundamental to why Bitcoin works. So I wanted to attempt making this concept accessible in the Bitcoin one pager.

Lessons from Writing Bitcoin 1 Million

Creating this new version, I incorporated knowledge and experience from writing the Bitcoin 1 Million book. These insights helped me distill complex ideas into clear, digestible explanations that work for complete beginners.

The Car Wash Inspiration

My goal with this? I actually want to sell these as physical sheets at my local car wash in Santa Monica. That’s what inspired version one - I can work from there while my car is being washed (it’s also walking distance to the office, but that’s beside the point).

The car wash is where I even updated it to version two. There’s something about making a resource that works in the real world - something you can hand to someone or leave on a counter.

Introducing BitcoinOnePager.com

I’ve created a new site: bitcoinonepager.com

It’s got:

  • The latest version 3 PDF available for free download
  • High-quality laminated prints you can order
  • Great information and links to continue learning
  • A sober, easy-to-understand starting place for Bitcoin education

Download or Order Prints

Visit bitcoinonepager.com to:

  • Download the free PDF and share it with anyone
  • Order laminated prints for yourself, events, or to share at your local spots
  • Find additional resources to continue learning

I hope this becomes a great resource for people to start their Bitcoin journey in a clear, accessible way. If you’ve been looking for something to share with friends, family, or curious newcomers - this is it.
